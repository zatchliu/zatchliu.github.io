## About

Hi, I'm **Zachary Liu**. I work at the intersection of software, data, design, and art, building things that run in the browser and in the shop.

Computer science coursework gave me a strong base in algorithms, optimization, and writing correct, efficient code. As an IDEAS major, I've turned ideas into physical objects: 3D printing, CNC routing, woodworking, soldering, and other fabrication workflows.
